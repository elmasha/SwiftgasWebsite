About Us

SwiftGas is a Moblie appliction that simpifies Buying or refilling of cooking gas, Users can locate nearby shops and order cooking gas.

  • Door step Delivery.
  • Cheap, convinient and reliable compared to other platforms
  • Saves you time.
  • Real time orders and Fast delivery.

Services

SwiftGas mobile app runs for 24 hours around the clock.

Locate nearby vendor buy or refill your cooking gas and have it swiftly delivered at home.

  • SwiftGas

    Mostly targets customers who want to buy or refill cooking gas and have it deliverd at home. The app locates nearby vendors who will recieve the customers requests.

  • SwiftVendor

    Particularly target gas vendors who supply cooking gas to houses. SwiftVendor enables the gas vendors upload product, map order location and manage shops.
